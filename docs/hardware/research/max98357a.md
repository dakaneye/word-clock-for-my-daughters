# MAX98357A I²S Class D Amplifier

## Part

- **Chip:** Maxim MAX98357A (now Analog Devices)
- **Breakout:** Adafruit Product ID 3006, or Chinese clones sold as "MAX98357 I²S 3W amplifier" (2-pack from GODIYMODULES on the wishlist is a clone)

## Verified facts

### Supply voltage
- **Vin range: 2.5 V to 5.5 V**
- **Recommended: 5 V for maximum output power** — even when driving 3.3 V logic devices.

### I²S logic levels
- **"3.3-5 V logic" tolerant** — verified explicitly by Adafruit. No level shifter needed between ESP32 (3.3 V) and the MAX98357A's BCLK/LRCLK/DIN pins.

### GAIN pin behavior
| GAIN pin state | Gain |
|---|---|
| Floating (unconnected) | **9 dB** (default) |
| Tied to GND | 12 dB |
| Tied to Vin | 6 dB |
| 100 kΩ to GND | 15 dB |
| 100 kΩ to Vin | 3 dB |

Power-cycle the amp after changing GAIN. Safe to leave floating for initial testing.

### SD (shutdown / channel select) pin behavior
- Has an **internal 100 kΩ pulldown**, so it's not truly floating.
- Adafruit breakout adds a **1 MΩ pullup to Vin**, producing **stereo-averaged output** at 5 V Vin.
- Without that external resistor (or with different rails), you may need to experiment to select left/right channel output.
- **Safe to leave SD unconnected** on the Adafruit board. For the clone on the wishlist, verify with a multimeter before power-up.

### Decoupling
- Adafruit guide does not explicitly state whether onboard decoupling caps are present. Assume yes on Adafruit, possibly missing on clones. Always safe to add 0.1 µF ceramic near the Vin pad.

## ESP32-specific notes

- No gotchas called out in Adafruit documentation.
- I²S pin assignment on ESP32 is software-configurable to any digital GPIO. This design uses BCLK=26, LRC=25, DIN=27 (see pinout.md).
